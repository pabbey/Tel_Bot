Tel Bot

    The revolution is here.

    Tel Bot is a telegram based chatbot which provides you with informations such as Weather report and time. It also assist you tu on and off lights in your home.  etc...


    SYSTEM MANUAL The programme contains three (3) functions. Thus,

    1. Weather (weather city country)

    2. Time (time)

    3. Home Automation. To put various lights on and off, follow these commands.
    a. (on bedroom light)
    b. (off bedroom light)
    c. (on living room light)
    d. (off living room light)
    e. (on garage light)
    f. (off garage light)
    g. (on outside light)
    h. (off outside light)
